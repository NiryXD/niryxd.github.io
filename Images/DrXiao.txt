Dr. Chunhong (Hong) Xiao completed her post-doctoral training in the StAR Lab at the UAB School of Nursing. She is now an Assistant Professor at the University of Tennessee at Knoxville. Her research focuses on care-resistant behaviors among people with dementia. She earned her BSN with Honors at UABSON and became an RN. She graduated with her PhD in Nursing and developed expertise in qualitative and quantitative methods for secondary data analysis, systematic review, and meta-analysis. In her postdoctoral training, she continues to build on her research on the characteristics and management of behavioral symptoms of dementia in diverse populations. Her research interests include AI applications in behavioral symptom science among persons living with dementia.
